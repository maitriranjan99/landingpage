Introducing Our Course: Crafting Compelling Narratives

Our 2-week online course is designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the world of journalism. Through a combination of lectures, case studies, and hands-on exercises, you'll learn how to:

  • Research and investigate
  • Conduct interviews with confidence
  • Write engaging and impactful stories
  • Navigate ethical dilemmas in journalism
  • Adapt to new media landscapes
